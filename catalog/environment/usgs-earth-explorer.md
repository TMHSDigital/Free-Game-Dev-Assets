# USGS EarthExplorer

Official USGS/EROS portal for Landsat, SRTM, and other Earth archives. USGS-authored scientific content is U.S. public domain — strong source for SRTM/3DEP-style heightmaps and satellite textures. Expect GIS reprojection/downsample before runtime use.

## Notes

- Free account may be required for downloads. Portal had scheduled maintenance notices in Jul 2026.
- **Third-party / copyrighted embeds** on USGS pages are marked separately — those need permission; do not assume every EarthExplorer hit is PD.
- SRTM 1″ media on USGS is explicitly labeled Public Domain. For reprocessed global DEMs prefer [nasadem](nasadem.md).

## Evidence

- Live USGS [Copyrights and Credits](https://www.usgs.gov/information-policies-and-instructions/copyrights-and-credits): USGS information/products “are considered to be in the U.S. Public Domain” (with noted exceptions for third-party material).
- Live EROS SRTM page / media: “Sources/Usage: Public Domain.”
