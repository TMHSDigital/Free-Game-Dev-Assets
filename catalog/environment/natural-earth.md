# Natural Earth

Public-domain vector + raster map data at 1:10m / 1:50m / 1:110m (coastlines, admin, physical). Ideal for stylized world maps, UI globes, and low-detail terrain masks — not high-res heightfields.

## Notes

- Credit optional (“Made with Natural Earth”) — not required.
- Pair with [openstreetmap](openstreetmap.md) / [osm2world](../tools/osm2world.md) when you need street-level detail.
