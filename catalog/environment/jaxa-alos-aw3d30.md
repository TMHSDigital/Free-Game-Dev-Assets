# JAXA ALOS AW3D30

Global ~30 m DSM from ALOS/PRISM. Free for commercial and non-commercial use under JAXA research-data terms — **© JAXA credit required**; **notify JAXA in advance for commercial use**. Optical DEM — cloud/snow voids possible. Complements USGS / Copernicus.

## Notes

- Download requires free AW3D30 account
- Credit examples: `Credit: AW3D30 (JAXA)` / `Provided by AW3D30 (Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency)`
- Commercial projects: follow §2.3 advance notification in Terms of Use of Research Data

## Evidence

- Live AW3D30 page (2026-07-19): “Any of the commercial and non-commercial purposes can be used free of charge under the conditions of the Terms of Use”
- Live [Terms of Use of Research Data](https://earth.jaxa.jp/en/data/policy/) (2026-07-19): use/modify/distribute without fee if credit JAXA; “The user can use the Research Data for commercial purposes without a fee; however, the user needs to notify JAXA thereof in advance.”
