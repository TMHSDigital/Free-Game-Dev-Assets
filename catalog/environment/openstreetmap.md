# OpenStreetMap

Global geodata under **ODbL**. Compiled games are usually “Produced Works” (binary not copylefted); redistributing a modified database can trigger share-alike. Credit OSM contributors. Related converter: [OSM2World](../tools/osm2world.md).

## Notes

- Required: attribution notice + make clear data is under ODbL ([copyright](https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright))
- Attribution example: `© OpenStreetMap contributors (ODbL)`
- See OSMF attribution guidelines for maps vs static embeds

## Evidence

- Live [Copyright and License](https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright) (2026-07-19): “OpenStreetMap is open data, licensed under the Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbL) … free to copy, distribute, transmit and adapt … as long as you credit OpenStreetMap and its contributors.”
