# Copernicus DEM GLO-30

Radar DSM ~30 m (TanDEM-X / WorldDEM lineage) via CDSE. **Full, Free & Open** licence for WorldDEM-30: free reproduction, distribution, public communication, and adaptation worldwide. Prefer over FABDEM (NC — high-risk).

## Notes

- Required credit when distributing / showing publicly: `© DLR e.V. 2010-2014 and © Airbus Defence and Space GmbH 2014-2018 provided under COPERNICUS by the European Union and ESA; all rights reserved.`
- Adapted data: prefix with `produced using Copernicus WorldDEM-30 …`
- GLO-90 has a parallel Free & Open licence; EEA-10 / WorldDEM-10 stay restricted.
- COP-DEM **view service** needs CCM user registration from **28 Jul 2026** — download/API paths still require CDSE account + CCM licence acceptance.
- Do not imply EU/ESA/Airbus endorsement.

## Evidence

- CDSE COP-DEM page: “GLO-30 and GLO-90 datasets are available worldwide with a free license” + DLR/Airbus credit notices.
- Live ESA CCM licence PDF (Issue 21/02/2025): “Full, Free & Open Licence for the use of the Copernicus WorldDEM-30” — Art. 4 rights (a–d) reproduction/distribution/public communication/adaptation; Art. 5 free of charge; Art. 3 worldwide.
