# Mixkit Sound Effects

Envato Mixkit free SFX library — royalty-free, **no attribution**, commercial OK (ads/YouTube/etc. per FAQ). No signup. Prefer dedicated game packs (Kenney/Sonniss) when you need footsteps-first libraries; Mixkit is a broad filler.

## Notes

- Read [Mixkit License](https://mixkit.co/license/) hub for item-type specifics (music vs SFX differ)
- Do not confuse free Mixkit SFX with Envato Elements paid catalog

## Evidence

- Live SFX hub (2026-07-19): “royalty-free and can be used without attribution … free sound effects license means you can use these sounds in both commercial and personal projects.” FAQ: “Yes, you can use Mixkit Sound Effects for commercial and personal projects.”
