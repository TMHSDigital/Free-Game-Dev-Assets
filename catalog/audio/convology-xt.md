# Convology XT Free Factory

Free convolution reverb plugin + **74** factory vintage IR samples (Impulse Record). No time limit / iLok. Product page sells the full 2,965-file library separately — clear plugin free tier, **unclear** whether factory IR WAVs may be extracted/embedded in a shipped game.

## Notes
