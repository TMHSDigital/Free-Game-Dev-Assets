# Freesound

Huge community audio library. Per-sound licenses are **CC0**, **CC BY**, or **CC BY-NC** (plus legacy Sampling+). Filter to **CC0** for commercial shipping — NC uploads are not commercial-safe.

## Notes

- Search filters are not a legal guarantee — open each sound’s license page before packaging
- Attribution list tool: freesound.org/home/attribution/
- Prefer [kenney-rpg-audio](kenney-rpg-audio.md) / [bigsoundbank](bigsoundbank.md) when you want site-wide commercial clarity

## Evidence

- Live [FAQ — Licenses](https://freesound.org/help/faq/) (2026-07-19): uploaders choose CC0 / BY / BY-NC; “Noncommercial… you can't earn any money with the piece of work you create!” → keep `needs-review` / `commercial: unknown` at catalog level
