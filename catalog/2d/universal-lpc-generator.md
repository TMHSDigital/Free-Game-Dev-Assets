# Universal LPC Spritesheet Generator

Composable Liberated Pixel Cup paper-doll sheets. Per-layer licenses include **CC0 / CC-BY / CC-BY-SA / OGA-BY / GPL** — generator exports a credit list. Commercial use OK if you honor the **strictest** licenses in your composition (often SA). Not a CC0 mega-pack. See [`docs/high-risk.md`](../../docs/high-risk.md).

## Notes

- Download CSV/plain-text credits from the tool when exporting
- Prefer layers that are CC0/CC-BY/OGA-BY when you must avoid SA viral terms
- Site: liberatedpixelcup.github.io; source: LiberatedPixelCup/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator
