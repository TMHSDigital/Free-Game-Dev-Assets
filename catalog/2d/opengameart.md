# OpenGameArt

Community hub for game art under many licenses (CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-SA, GPL, OGA-BY, etc.). Filter aggressively — treat each upload as its own license review. Prefer catalogued primaries ([glitch-archive](glitch-archive.md), [lpc-revised-basics](lpc-revised-basics.md)) when possible.

## Notes

- FAQ summarizes per-license commercial/attribution rules; multi-license uploads = pick one
- CC-BY-NC / SA packs are common — do not assume commercial-safe from “free download”

## Evidence

- Live [FAQ — What do the licenses mean?](https://opengameart.org/content/faq) (2026-07-19): CC0 “commercial use is ok”; CC-BY requires credit; site hosts mixed licenses → keep catalog-level `needs-review`
