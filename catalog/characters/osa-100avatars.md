# Open Source Avatars (100Avatars CC0)

Curated VRM avatar registry. The original **100Avatars** series (R1–R3) is **CC0** and meant for games/VR without attribution. Prefer those collections over mixed community packs.

## Notes

- Registry metadata/repo is CC0; **per-collection license** lives in `data/projects.json` — always filter by license before shipping.
- Community collections include CC-BY and CC0 NFT-origin sets; treat non-100Avatars rows as separate reviews.
- VRM pipeline: UniVRM (Unity), three-vrm (web), VRM4U (Unreal).

## Evidence

- Live `projects.json`: `100Avatars R1/R2/R3` → `license: CC0`.
- Site license guide: CC0 = commercial use, no attribution required.
- Repo `LICENSE`: registry CC0; avatars per collection.
