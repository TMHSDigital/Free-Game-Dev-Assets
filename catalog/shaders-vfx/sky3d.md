# Sky3D

Godot 4 atmospheric day/night cycle (sun/moon/stars, fog, clouds, exposure). MIT addon — keep notice with redistributed copies. Third-party assets under `assets/thirdparty` have separate licenses — check before shipping those files.

## Notes

- Copy `addons/sky_3d` into your project and enable the plugin
- Godot 4.3+; Forward / Mobile / Compatibility (compat needs sky/fog tweaks per README)
